# Outreach Research Guide — How to find 60 schools in 3 hours

## Goal
Fill all 60 rows of `school-prospect-list.csv` with real, contactable schools in your city.

## Method (3 sessions of 1 hour each)

### Session 1 — Bulk discovery (60 schools in 1 hour)

| Source | Action | Yield |
|---|---|---|
| **Google Maps** | Search `private schools [your city]`. Scroll through ALL results. Click each pin. | 30-40 schools |
| **Google Search** | Search `"top private schools in [your city]"`, `"NAPPS [state] members"`, `"best schools in [area]"` | +10-15 schools |
| **Facebook search** | Search `[your city] private school`. Click the Pages tab. | +5-10 schools |
| **NAPPS Directory** | https://napps.org.ng → state chapter → member list | +10-20 schools |
| **Word of mouth** | Ask your existing maintenance client if they know other schools | +3-5 referrals |

**For each school, capture into CSV:**
- School Name
- Location / Area
- Type (Private / Public / International / Faith-based)
- Estimated size (Small <200, Medium 200-500, Large 500+)

### Session 2 — Find decision makers (1 hour)

For each school in your list:

1. **Google:** `"[School Name] principal"` or `"[School Name] proprietor"`
2. **LinkedIn search:** `Principal at [School Name]` or `Head Teacher at [School Name]`
3. **Facebook:** Find the school's page → check "About" tab → often lists director/proprietor
4. **School website:** Look for "About Us" / "Our Team" / "Leadership" page

**Capture:**
- Decision Maker Name (Principal, Proprietor, Director, Head Teacher)
- Role
- LinkedIn URL

### Session 3 — Get contact info + observe pain (1 hour)

For each school:

1. **WhatsApp:** Most schools list WhatsApp on Facebook/Instagram bio
2. **Email:** Usually `info@[school].edu.ng` or on the website Contact page
3. **Visit their website:** Look for these specific pain points:
   - ❌ No website at all → biggest opportunity
   - ❌ Outdated design (looks like 2010) → easy pitch
   - ❌ No online fee payment → instant value-add
   - ❌ No parent portal → instant value-add
   - ❌ No mobile-responsive → 80% of parents browse on phone
   - ❌ Generic Facebook page only → upgrade them
4. **Capture observed pain point** in the CSV (this becomes your DM opening line)

## Prioritization

After research, sort the CSV by these tiers:

| Priority | Criteria | Why |
|---|---|---|
| **1-20 (Hot)** | Already has an outdated website | They understand value, just need an upgrade. **Easiest close.** |
| **21-40 (Warm)** | Active social media, no proper website | They invest in marketing, will invest in a website |
| **41-60 (Cold)** | No online presence | Biggest impact but longest sales cycle |

**Hit priorities 1-20 first.** Schools that already paid for a website once are 3x more likely to pay again.

## Daily routine

| Time | Action | Duration |
|---|---|---|
| 9:00 AM | Send 3 personalized DMs (use templates above, customize first line) | 15 min |
| Throughout day | Reply to any responses within 30 min | 10 min |
| 6:00 PM | Update CSV `STATUS` and `LAST ACTION DATE` | 5 min |
| **Total daily** | | **30 min** |

## STATUS values to use in CSV

- `Not Contacted` — research done, not reached out yet
- `DM Sent` — initial message sent, awaiting reply
- `Replied` — they responded, conversation active
- `Demo Booked` — discovery call scheduled
- `Proposal Sent` — sent pricing/scope
- `Closed Won` 🎉
- `Closed Lost` — no for now, revisit in 6 months
- `No Response` — sent 2 follow-ups, no reply → archive
